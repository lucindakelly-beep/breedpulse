HWSD Testing

Why HWSD status matters

Hoof Wall Separation Disease (HWSD) is a genetic condition specific to the Connemara breed. A mare owner breeding from a Carrier stallion with a Carrier mare risks producing an Affected foal. Every stallion in the BreedPulse directory has a clearly displayed HWSD status — it is the most important health information a mare owner needs before booking a covering.

HWSD status is sourced directly from the submitted test certificate or from studbook records. We do not publish a stallion's profile until HWSD status is confirmed.

How we verify stallion profiles

Every stallion on BreedPulse is verified against recognised studbook records before their profile is published. Profiles that cannot be verified are not published.

We currently accept registrations from the following studbooks:

CPBS BCPS HSI ACPS (Australia) NZCPS (New Zealand) CPAA (USA) CPCSA (South Africa) CPCS (Sweden) CPCDN (Denmark) CPCN (Netherlands)
