How BreedPulse works

A verified directory built for mare owners and stallion owners alike. Here's exactly what we do and how.

  • Search and filter the full directory for free
  • View HWSD status, pedigree, and sport record
  • Filter by country, height, colour, studbook, discipline
  • Filter by covering method — Natural, Chilled AI, Frozen AI
  • Get a Season Pass to unlock stud fees and booking contacts

  • Your stallion verified before going live

For mare owners

Finding your stallion, step by step

1

Browse the directory for free

The full BreedPulse directory is free to search and filter. No account required. Use the filters to narrow by HWSD status, country, height, colour, studbook, discipline, and covering method.

2

Review stallion profiles

Each stallion profile shows pedigree (sire, dam, dam's sire), foaling year, sport record, HWSD status badge, studbook registration, and covering methods — all visible for free.

3

Get a Season Pass to unlock details

A Season Pass (€9.99 for 90 days) reveals stud fees, covering dates and availability, mare requirements, and the direct booking contact for every stallion in the directory.

4

Book directly with the stud

We give you the contact — you take it from there. No commission, no booking fees. BreedPulse connects you directly with the stallion owner or stud manager.

Our verification process

What we check before a stallion goes live

HWSD Test Result

We confirm the official HWSD test result — Clear (N/N), Carrier (N/Hw), or Affected. No self-reported results accepted.

Studbook Registration

CPBS, BCPS, and international studbook registrations are checked and confirmed before the listing goes live.

Covering Details

Covering methods, availability, and season information are confirmed for the current 2026 covering season.

Licence Class

Where applicable, CPBS licence class is verified and displayed on the stallion card.
