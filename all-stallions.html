We've built this register from public records and breed society directories, so there will inevitably be small details that need correcting — wrong year, wrong height, sold to a new owner, retired from stud. A note on this list. Entries marked Listed have been submitted and verified directly with the owner. Everything else is drawn from public records, breed society directories and community knowledge — so details may be incomplete, out of date, or in a few cases inaccurate. If you see something that doesn't look right, or a pony you'd like removed, drop us a line at hello@breedpulse.com and we'll put it right.
