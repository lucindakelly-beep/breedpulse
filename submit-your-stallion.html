What you'll need before you start

  • Stallion's registered name and studbook number
  • HWSD test certificate or studbook status confirmation
  • Licence class and current licence year
  • Height, colour, and pedigree details
  • Covering methods offered for 2026
  • Contact for booking enquiries (members only)

  • Photo (optional but recommended)

What happens after you submit

1

Submit your profile

Complete the submission form — takes around 10 minutes. No account required.

2

Verification within 3 working days

We check your stallion's details against studbook records. Profiles that cannot be verified are not published.

3

You're notified when live

You'll receive a direct message when your profile is published and searchable in the directory.

Your privacy is protected. Stud fees, covering details, and your booking contact are shown to paying members only — they are never displayed publicly on the directory.
