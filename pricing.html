Questions & answers

What does the Season Pass cover?

A Season Pass gives you full access to stud fees, covering dates, mare requirements, and direct booking contact for every verified stallion in the directory, valid for 90 days from purchase — covering the full 2026 season.

Is the directory really free to browse?

Yes — searching, filtering, and viewing stallion profiles including pedigree, HWSD status, height, colour, and disciplines is completely free. Only the commercial booking details require a pass.

How do stallions get verified?

Every stallion on BreedPulse is manually verified by our team before going live — HWSD test results confirmed, studbook registration checked, and covering details confirmed for the current season.

Can I list my stallion on BreedPulse?

Yes — listing your stallion is free. We'll verify your stallion's details before the listing goes live. Submit your stallion here →
